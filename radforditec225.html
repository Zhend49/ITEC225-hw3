To calculate dissplacement we will use the formula D=vi*t-(1/2)a*t^2 which is Kinematic Physic Equation

Where:

  • D = Displacement
  • vi = initial velocity m/s^2
  • t = time in seconds s
  • a = acceleration m/s^2
